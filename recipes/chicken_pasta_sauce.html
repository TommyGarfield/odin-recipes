Chicken Tomatato Pasta Sauce

Description

A quick and easy chicken and tomato sauce, the perfect high protein, high nutrient addition to your pasta (other carbs are available).

A quick 30 minute recipe!

Ingredients

  • 400g of Diced Chicken Breast
  • 1/2 A Large White Onion
  • 3-5 Cloves of Garlic
  • 600g of Tinned Tomatos
  • 2TBSPs of Tomato Puree/Paste
  • 1-2Tbsp EVOO
  • 2 Tsp Dried Oregano
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Place up a medium sized saucepan on a medium-high heat.
  2. Add the EVOO once the pan is sufficiently warm.
  3. Add the onion and stir occasionally until golden brown.
  4. Add the garlic and chicken, stiring occasionally.
  5. Once the chicken is no longer pink add the chopped tomatos, tomato paste, oregano, salt and pepper and stir.
  6. Let it simmer on a medium heat for 10 minutes without a lid.
  7. Add a lid turn down the heat and let it simmer for another 30 mniutes.
  8. Stir and taste occasionally, add more salt/pepper when and if neccessary.
  9. Turn off the heat and leave it for now.
  10. Cook your pasta and strain.
  11. Add desired amount of sauce to the now empty pasta pot, put it on a low heat for a couple of miuntes.
  12. Meanwhile add your pasta to a bowl and drizle with EVOO and a pinch of salt, then stir.
  13. Add this pasta to the sauce and combine.
  14. Serve and garnish with fresh herbs or cheese if desired.
