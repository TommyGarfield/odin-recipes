Chicken and Fried Egg Burger

Description

A quick and easy chicken burger with fried egg, a great high protein, high nutrient meal for any time of day.

A quick 30 minute recipe!

Ingredients

  • 150g Whole Chicken Breast
  • ~3 slices of a Large Red Onion
  • 1 Egg
  • Lettuce Leaves
  • 2TBSP of Mayonnaise
  • 1-2Tbsp EVOO
  • 1 Burger Bun
  • 2-3 Slices of Tomato.
  • Dry Rub Grilled Chicken Powder
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash the chicken and, on a chopping board, add desired amount of dry rub grilled chicken powder and salt.
  2. Place a medium sized frying on a medium-high heat.
  3. Add the EVOO once the pan is sufficiently warm.
  4. Add the chicken and make sure both sides recieved sufficient oil.
  5. On a medium-high heat fry for ~5minutes on each side, until golden and slightly charred.
  6. Reduce the heat slightly and add the onion circles, this can be done during the chicken cooking if desired.
  7. Remove the chicken and onion and add to your lightly toasted burger bun.
  8. Top with mayonnaise, lettuce and tomato.
  9. Add your egg to the frying pan, with more oil if neccessary. Fry for desired time.
  10. Add the egg to the burger and close with the top of the bun.
  11. Serve.
