Plant Based Protein Shake

Description

A high calorie plant based protein shake, made with easy to aquire, healthy ingredients.

A quick 10 minutes recipe!

Ingredients

  • ~50g of Vegan Protein Powder(We use Optimum Nutrition's choloclate flavoured powder)
  • 80g of Organic Rolled Oats
  • 30g of Pumpkin Seeds
  • 50g of 100% Peanut Butter
  • 100g of Organic Greek Yogurt
  • 1 Whole Banana
  • ~900ml Water

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients to a blender
  2. Blend for ~1 minute
  3. Serve and enjoy
  4. Note: If too thick add more water.
